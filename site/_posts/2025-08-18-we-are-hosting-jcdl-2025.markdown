NIU is proud to host the [25th ACM/IEEE Joint Conference on Digital Libraries (JCDL 2025)](https://2025.jcdl.org/) from December 15-19, 2025. This prestigious conference will take place at the NIU Naperville campus, bringing together researchers, practitioners, and educators from around the world to discuss the latest advancements in digital libraries and related fields.
