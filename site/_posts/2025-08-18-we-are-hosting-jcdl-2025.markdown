NIU is proud to host the [25th ACM/IEEE Joint Conference on Digital Libraries (JCDL 2025)](https://2025.jcdl.org/) from December 15-19, 2025. This prestigious conference will take place virtually this year, bringing together researchers, practitioners, and educators from around the world to discuss the latest advancements in digital libraries and related fields.
The registration for JCDL 2025 is now open! We encourage all interested individuals to register early to secure their spot at this exciting event. For more information on registration fees, deadlines, and other details, please visit the [JCDL 2025 Registration Page](https://2025.jcdl.org/registration/).
